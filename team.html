Meet the Team

We are an interdisciplinary team blending synthetic biology, computation, and design to make RNA origami practical and impactful.

Team Structure

  • Wet Lab: Build and characterize RNA folding constructs.
  • Dry Lab: Simulate secondary structure and optimize sequences.
  • Human Practices: Integrate stakeholder needs and ethics.
  • Communication: Turn complex RNA design into clear stories.

What Drives Us

We want biology to be engineered with the same elegance as architecture: form and function designed together.

What We’re Building

A reusable RNA origami toolkit that future teams can adapt to new diagnostics and therapeutic challenges.
