Project Abstract

Our platform engineers single-stranded RNA to self-assemble into precise nanoscale geometries with programmable docking sites for biosensing, molecular imaging, and targeted response systems.

By combining computational folding design with iterative lab validation, we create reusable RNA components that can be adapted quickly for new biological contexts.

Design Pillars

A coherent scientific product vision across sequence design, modularity, and translational relevance.

Fold Logic

Encode structural behavior directly in sequence architecture to produce predictable nanoscale forms.

Functional Modules

Attach sensing motifs, binders, and reporters without compromising structural integrity.

Rapid Iteration

Use model-informed experiments to improve each generation with clear measurable criteria.

Style & Motion Brief

Quality bar for the full website redesign and animation consistency.

Visual Language

  • Color hierarchy: deep space backgrounds, high-contrast text, cyan-violet accent signals.
  • Typography scale: compact readable body with bold, cinematic headline treatment.
  • Spatial rhythm: consistent vertical spacing and panelized content blocks.
  • Shape system: medium-large radii and soft borders for a modern lab interface look.
  • Shadow treatment: layered depth without heavy visual noise.

Motion Tone

  • Ambient: slow background drift to maintain energy without distraction.
  • Reveal: gentle scroll-based entrance for reading flow.
  • Micro-interaction: precise hover/focus responses on links, cards, and media.
  • Safety: reduced-motion users receive static, immediate content.
  • Performance: transforms/opacity prioritized over expensive animated properties.

Visual QA Acceptance Checklist

Cross-page quality gates for consistency, motion, accessibility, and responsive polish.

  • Typography, spacing, and component behavior remain consistent on all pages.
  • Animations are subtle, coherent, and free of jank or overlap issues.
  • Keyboard focus states and contrast remain visible across interactive elements.
  • Reduced-motion mode disables non-essential animation and transitions.
  • Mobile, tablet, and desktop layouts preserve readability and hierarchy.
