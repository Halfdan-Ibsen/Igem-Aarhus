Integrated Human Practices

We build RNA origami with society, not just for society.

Stakeholder Interviews

We interviewed RNA biologists, clinicians, and biotech innovators to prioritize real use-cases: low-cost diagnostics, programmable therapeutics, and scalable manufacturing.

"If teams can make RNA structures both robust and modular, this could transform how we prototype molecular devices." - Stakeholder feedback

Public Engagement & Education

We translated RNA nanotechnology into visual storytelling through school outreach, open-lab demos, and interactive workshops on how structure drives biological function.

Ethics and Safety

We evaluate dual-use concerns, containment, and translational ethics from day one. Our framework emphasizes non-pathogenic systems, transparent communication, and responsible deployment pathways.
